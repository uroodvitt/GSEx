==============================
HOW TO CHANGE ICON
==============================
Note:After you create macro in GSE, that macro is just a template not an actual Macro, to use them you need to 
1- click "Create Icon"
   you will see icon changed to Question Mark
2- Drag that Question Mark Button to Your Bar. (GSE will automatically create a Macro)
3- Now go to Your Macros: /macro
4- Modify the icon/Name to your liking.
